🌱 Planty

Smart plant care for indoor plants

🪴 How it works

Place your pot (with drainage holes) in a tray or container filled with 1-2 inches of water. The soil will absorb moisture from below through capillary action.

⏱️ Takes 15-30 minutes

Let your plant sit in the water for 15-30 minutes until the top of the soil feels damp. Then remove from water and let it drain.

🚫 Don't water again until

The top 1-2 inches of soil feels completely dry. Stick your finger in to check! This usually takes several days depending on the plant.

✨ Benefits

  • Encourages deeper, stronger root growth
  • Prevents overwatering & root rot
  • Plants absorb only what they need
  • Keeps leaves dry (prevents fungal disease)
  • More even moisture distribution

⚠️ This App Blocks Watering for 24 Hours

To prevent overwatering, Planty won't let you record another watering within 24 hours. This gives soil time to absorb water properly.

🪦 Plant Memorial

Plants that didn't make it are remembered here. Their learning data is preserved forever.

🧠 How Learning Works

When plants die, Planty learns and adjusts the watering schedule for future plants of the same type:

💧 Overwatering Death

Interval increases by 25-30% (water less often)

🏜️ Underwatering Death

Interval decreases by 20-25% (water more often)

🔄 Multiple Deaths (Same Plant)

Each death adds more adjustment - the more data, the smarter the schedule

⚖️ Mixed Causes

If deaths have different causes, Planty finds a balanced middle ground
