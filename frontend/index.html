🪴 How it works

Place your pot (with drainage holes) in a tray filled with 1-2 inches of water. The soil absorbs moisture from below.

⏱️ Takes 15-30 minutes

Let your plant sit until the top soil feels damp. Then remove and let drain.

✨ Benefits

  • Deeper, stronger root growth
  • Prevents overwatering & root rot
  • Even moisture distribution

⚠️ 24-Hour Cooldown

To prevent overwatering, Planty blocks watering for 24 hours after each session.
